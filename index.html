About Us

A community built on
growth & connection.

MSCC started as a small Discord server with a handful of Mapua students who just wanted a place to talk outside of class. Three years later, we've grown into one of the most active unofficial student communities at the university — over 3,000 members strong, all student-run, zero institutional backing.

🎓

Who We Are

Students, by students. No faculty oversight, no school branding — just a group of Mapua people building something they actually want to be part of.

💬

What We Do

Academic discussion channels, monthly art showcases, study pod matchmaking, game nights, and a whole lot of memes in between. There's a channel for everyone.

🌐

Open to Everyone

Freshman still figuring out NSTP, or a senior about to defend — MSCC is open to all Mapua students, no gatekeeping, no requirements.
