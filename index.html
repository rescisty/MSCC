About Us

🎓

Who We Are

MSCC (Mapua Student Community Circle) is the official Discord-based community for Mapua University students — a space to connect, collaborate, and grow beyond the classroom.

💬

What We Do

We host academic discussions, creative showcases, community events, and peer support channels — all built around the Mapua experience.

🌐

Join Us

Open to all Mapua students. Whether you're a freshman finding your footing or a senior sharing wisdom — there's a place for you here.
