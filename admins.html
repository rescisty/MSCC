The Backbone

This server only lives
because of them.

MSCC doesn't run on algorithms or automated systems — it runs on people. Eleven students who give their time, energy, and creativity to make this community worth showing up to every day.

Behind every event announcement, every moderated channel, every art showcase, and every study pod — there's a person who cared enough to make it happen. They don't do it for clout. They do it because they believe Mapua students deserve a space that feels like home.
